Red flags hiding in plain sight

These patterns appear in the P&L before they surface anywhere else.
🚩
Revenue up. PAT flat or falling.

Costs or interest are eating the gains. Dig into the line items.
🚩
EBITDA positive. PAT negative.

Debt or depreciation is wiping out the operating gains entirely.
🚩
Margins compressing, year after year.

Gross margin falls even as revenue grows. Pricing power is eroding.
