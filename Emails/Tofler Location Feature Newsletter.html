Due Diligence Checklist

5 questions to ask before you trust any company's claims.
Does the company actually manufacture or just trade?
Does their geographic reach match their revenue claims?
Are all operations in one state? Single-state = single point of failure.
Do location types match the business model? No factory for a manufacturer is a red flag.
Do they operate near where they claim to serve? Verify before finalising any vendor.
