Our Story: How It All Began

A decade of empowering Indian SMEs, startups, and enterprises with transparency, reliability,
and data-backed business intelligence.

2013

Identifying the gap: Indian SMEs and startups lacked reliable, structured, and easily accessible company data.

2014

Started distributing comprehensive reports directly via leading banks and key strategic partners.

2015

Launched Tofler's first web platform to drastically simplify and democratize instant access to financial reports.

2016

Focused heavily on engineering high-end due diligence reports tailored for top corporate investors.

2017

Realised SMEs were left out of the data revolution, shifting core product focus directly to SMEs.

2018

Successfully partnered with major corporate brands and industry leaders across the country.

2020

Introduced the revolutionary Company360 platform, delivering unparalleled instant insights.

2024

Launched Tofler Pro and Tofler Growth plans for high-tier advanced risk predictive analytics.

Present

Proudly stand as India's leading SME business intelligence platform, trusted daily by 150,000+ active users.

Smarter Strategic Decisions

Using Tofler’s risk assessment, industry insights, and financial analysis, TeamComputers reduced risk exposure and accelerated data-backed growth initiatives.

“Adding Tofler to our toolkit significantly enhanced our understanding of businesses and industries. It’s a game-changer.”

Read Full Story
Challenge
At TeamComputers we were facing challenges in the Indian markets while doing business research about a company because in the case the customers asking for credit period, analyzing them without checking the legal background, holding merger and subsidiary company and credit assessment for the company was a challenge. For this purpose we required a platform to conduct business research for an accurate decision making.
Solution
After utmost hunt, we found the platform Tofler Company360 and on boarded into our operations for Business Research and Analysis. We found the following information and insights useful for our purpose:
  • Company Information: Gives a detailed overview of a company, including its history, key personnel, subsidiaries, and contact information.
  • Financial Analysis: Access to financial statements, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements. We could analyze financial ratios and performance metrics for assessment in no time.
  • Risk Assessment: Provides us with potential risks associated with the company, including financial risks, legal issues, and market risks. Saved us a ton of efforts and time.
  • Industry Insights: Helps with industry-specific data, trends, and benchmark analysis to understand the company's position within its sector.
  • User-Friendly Interface: A user-friendly dashboard and interface designed to make complex data easy to understand and navigate.
Conclusion
Adding Tofler Company 360 to our toolkit has significantly enhanced our understanding of businesses and industries. Its comprehensive features provide a wealth of information and insights crucial for making informed decisions. With access to detailed financial analysis, competitor comparisons, risk assessments, industry-specific data, and customizable reports, it offers a holistic view of companies. The platform’s user-friendly interface makes navigating complex data effortless, while its real-time alerts ensure we stay updated and seamlessly incorporate insights into our workflows.

“Through Tofler, our business needs for consistent, time-saving data are finally being met.”

Read Full Story
Challenge
At Ratnabali, being a proprietary investment firm we are constantly reviewing data on companies, including the unlisted vertical. We are dependent on instant financial data of companies and a platform to help us retrieve them.
Solution
We decided to use Tofler's Company360 Enterprise solution as this product offers a user-friendly interface to review important financial data.
  • Flexible Financial Aid: Financial information is crucial in our work. Tofler's platform allows us to access a company's financial information at ease as made available on MCA. The detailed balance sheet, cash flows and profit and loss statements spread across multiple years makes the breakup easier to follow as provided in the original filed documents.
  • Capital Structure: While examining companies, details regarding known shareholders, capital history and allotments of funds are one of our requirements. Tofler provides a dedicated feature for this.
  • Industry Segmentation: Tofler's screener aid is highly helpful for evaluating companies for market research based on specific filtration like location, product, revenues, etc. providing instant research results.
  • Specialised Networking: Finding directorial details and establishing common connections between companies and directors is very important for our findings. Tofler's own Company Network tool quickly explores these relationships.
Conclusion
Our business constantly revolves around data-driven decisions for which a consistent and time-saving platform is a must. Through Tofler's inclusion, these business needs are being met.
