About ToflerGPT

ToflerGPT is an AI-powered intelligence layer built on top of Tofler’s proprietary company data platform, turning complex business data into instant, conversational insights.

Instead of manually analyzing financials and filings, users can simply ask questions and get structured data in seconds, ensuring faster and smarter decision making.

It is especially powerful for use cases like credit risk assessment, vendor analysis, client discovery, and peer comparison.
